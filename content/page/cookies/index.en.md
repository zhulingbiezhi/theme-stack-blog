# Cookie Policy

Effective Date: September 26, 2025

## What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or work more efficiently, and to provide information to website owners.

## How We Use Cookies

We use Cookies to:

### Essential Function Cookies
- **Session Management**: Maintain your browsing session
- **Security**: Prevent cross-site request forgery attacks
- **Website Functions**: Remember your preference settings (such as theme mode)

### Analytics Cookies
- **Google Analytics**: Understand how visitors use our website
- **Baidu Analytics**: Collect website usage data
- **Busuanzi Counter**: Count page views

### Advertising Cookies
- **Google AdSense**: Display relevant ads
- **Personalized Ads**: Show ads based on your interests

## Specific Cookies Used

### First-Party Cookies (Set by Us)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|------------|---------|----------|------|
| theme | Remember theme preference | 1 year | Functional |
| lang | Remember language preference | 1 year | Functional |

### Third-Party Cookies

#### Google Analytics
- **Purpose**: Website analysis and statistics
- **Cookie Types**: _ga, _ga_*, _gid
- **Duration**: 2 years (_ga), 1 day (_gid)
- **Data Collection**: Anonymous visit statistics

#### Google AdSense
- **Purpose**: Ad delivery and personalization
- **Cookie Types**: IDE, DSID, FLC, AID, TAID
- **Duration**: 1-2 years varies
- **Data Collection**: Ad preferences and behavior

#### Baidu Analytics
- **Purpose**: Visit statistics for China region
- **Cookie Types**: HMACCOUNT, BAIDUID
- **Duration**: 2 years
- **Data Collection**: Visit statistics data

## Managing Cookies

You can manage Cookies through:

### Browser Settings

#### Chrome Browser
1. Click three-dot menu in upper right
2. Select "Settings" > "Privacy and security"
3. Click "Cookies and other site data"
4. Choose your preference settings

#### Firefox Browser
1. Click menu button in upper right
2. Select "Settings" > "Privacy & Security"
3. Configure in "Cookies and Site Data" section

#### Safari Browser
1. Select "Safari" > "Preferences"
2. Click "Privacy" tab
3. Configure in "Cookies and website data" section

#### Edge Browser
1. Click three-dot menu in upper right
2. Select "Settings" > "Cookies and site permissions"
3. Click "Cookies and site data"

### Opt-Out of Specific Services

#### Google Analytics
- Install [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

#### Google Ads
- Visit [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- Turn off "Ad personalization"

#### Baidu Analytics
- Disable third-party Cookies in browser

## Impact of Cookies on Website Functions

### Disabling All Cookies
- Basic website functions still available
- Cannot remember your preference settings
- Some features may not work properly

### Disabling Analytics Cookies
- Does not affect website functions
- We cannot understand website usage
- Cannot improve user experience

### Disabling Advertising Cookies
- Ads will still be displayed
- Ads may not be relevant to your interests
- May see more repetitive ads
