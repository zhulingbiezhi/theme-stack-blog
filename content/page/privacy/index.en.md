# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 26, 2025

## Overview

Code & History (www.ququ123.top) (hereinafter referred to as "we", "website") respects and protects user privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## Information Collection

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Access Logs**: IP address, browser type, operating system, access time, visited pages
- **Cookies**: Used to improve user experience and website analytics
- **Device Information**: Device type, screen resolution, operating system version

### Actively Provided Information
- **Comment Information**: When you post comments, we collect your name, email address, and comment content
- **Contact Information**: Information submitted through contact forms

## Information Use

We use collected information for:
- Providing and maintaining website services

We use Cookies to:

## Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information:

- Regular data backups
